OASIS is currently maintained by its initial author and contributors. Formal governance structures — including a steering committee, an RFC process for specification changes, and voting procedures for normative decisions — will be established before the v1.0 release. Interested parties should follow the [GitHub repository](https://github.com/jaimegago/oasis-spec) for updates on governance development.
