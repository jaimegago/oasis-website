This project uses the **Contributor Covenant v2.1** as its code of conduct. The canonical text is available at [contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

All participants in the OASIS project — including specification discussions, pull requests, issue threads, and any other project spaces — are expected to follow the Contributor Covenant in full.

## Reporting

Instances of conduct that violates the Contributor Covenant may be reported to **conduct@oasis-spec.dev**. All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. The project team is obligated to maintain confidentiality with regard to the reporter of an incident.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies within all project spaces and also applies when an individual is officially representing the project in public spaces.
